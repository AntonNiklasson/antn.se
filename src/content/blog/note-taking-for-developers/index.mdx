---
title: Note-taking for developers
date: 2026-04-29
---

The habit of note-taking as a developer can really help in the daily work. It
will help you pick deep work up after a long weekend, remember the solution to a
weird bug 10 months ago and even help you grow and evolve as a developer.

As I've been pair programming a lot, I've found it really helpful to keep a
running document, or a journal, together with each colleague I'm working with.
Not only does this enable more of a mindful approach to tackling topics to work
on, you can also look back after a few months and remember the problems you
solved together. I think this could be a great tool when coaching more junior
developers.
