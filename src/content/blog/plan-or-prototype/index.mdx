---
title: Plan or prototype
date: 2026-02-25
---

I recent realisation I had, given all the craziness with AI, is that we should solve problems in one of two ways:
1. Plan: gather context, ask for input, plan in detail
2. Prototype: build first, analyse later

There's no in between anymore. Just either we go all in on planning and aligning ideas with ADRs and RFCs - or we do the complete opposite and just build a couple of prototypes.

This mindset is helping me making sense of it all. Not all problems require hours of detailed planning and consensus from the tribe. Some problems deserve to be built on two different assumptions, and then evaluated and discussed.
